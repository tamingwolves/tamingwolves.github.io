About taming Wolves

Taming Wolves is an indepentent game studio, that is based in Michigan, who makes indie games!

Game making is what our creative outlet is, and we want you to play the games we make!

We are currently developing a 2D platformer game. The game is currently being made with the Unity engine. The game doesn't not have a title yet, but it will later on! This game will consist of three different environments. The first environment will be a forest. The forest is a deadly land filled with unknown critter that will tear you to shreds if you're not careful enough. Also, the forest is riddle with many traps that even the most observant adventurer will not notice. Deep in the forest, is an entrance to a mythical cave that is rumored to be filled with many treasures. The only problem is that it's guarded by a magnificent beast known as "The Watcher". Once it's defeated, you'll be able to venture in this majestic cave. But beware, the cave is even more dangerous than the forest. Many people have stepped foot into the cave, but none have ever returned, alive. The ones that came back were mangled up corpses discarded by the creatures that lurk in the caves. Now you're probably wondering, what is the third environment? Well, that will be up to you to figure out when the game comes out!
